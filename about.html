Company Overview

BenHamaden is a distinguished international export and import enterprise headquartered in Republic of Chad, with an established presence in global trade since 1970.

With over five decades of operational excellence, BenHamaden delivers comprehensive A-to-Z export and import solutions, enabling seamless trade across international markets.
